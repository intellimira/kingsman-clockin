Data & Privacy Notice

UK GDPR — Required Before Use

What We Collect

  • Badge ID — Your Kingsman employee badge number
  • Venue — Which site you're assigned to (self-selected)
  • Timestamps — When you clock in and out

Why We Collect It

To verify your attendance at the assigned venue for payroll and client reporting. This is job verification, not performance monitoring.

How Long We Keep It

Shift records are retained for 24 months, then automatically deleted. You can request deletion at any time.

Who Can See It

  • You (your own shift history)
  • Your managers (John & Graham) — for payroll verification

Your Rights

  • Access — See all data we hold about you
  • Export — Download your shift history as a CSV file
  • Deletion — Request we delete all your data
  • Complaint — Contact the ICO if you have concerns

Lawful Basis

Processing is based on legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR) for job verification and payroll accuracy. A Legitimate Interests Assessment is on file.
